# Splunk Learning Hub

Your study home for the Splunk Core User course — eight lessons, a hands-on labs portal, search, and progress tracking, all in one place. It works in any modern browser and can be installed on your device so it opens like a regular app.

That is all you need to start. Bookmark it, or install it (below) so it is one tap away.

## Install it on your device (optional)

Installing lets the hub open full-screen from your home screen or desktop, and previously opened pages keep working without a connection.

- **Desktop (Chrome or Edge):** click **Install app** in the top bar, then confirm. (You can also use the install icon in the address bar.)
- **Android (Chrome):** tap the **⋮** menu, then **Install app** or **Add to Home screen**.
- **iPhone / iPad (Safari):** tap the **Share** button, then **Add to Home Screen**.

## How to use it

- **Home dashboard.** The "Pick up where you left off" card takes you to your next lesson. The ring shows how much of the course you have completed.
- **Lessons.** Each lesson is a card. Tap **Open lesson** to read it. Tap **Mark done** when you finish, and use the **star** to bookmark lessons you want to return to. Your status is saved automatically.
- **Practice Labs.** Open the labs portal for 48 hands-on exercises that follow the lessons.
- **Search lessons.** Use the search bar at the top (or press **/** on a keyboard) to jump to any lesson by title, topic, or concept.
- **Web Search.** Look up anything beyond the course. Your search opens in a new tab, focused on Splunk topics.
- **Light or dark.** Use the sun/moon button in the top bar to switch themes.

## Good to know

- Your progress and bookmarks are saved **only on your own device** — nothing is uploaded or shared.
- Because progress is stored per device, using a different browser or device starts a fresh record.
- Clearing your browser data will reset your saved progress.
